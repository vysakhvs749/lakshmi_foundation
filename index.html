Lakshmi Vigyan Foundation

Welcome to Lakshmi Vigyan Foundation, where our intention is to bridge the gap between health and education aiming to create a healthier and cognizant global community. Our vision is to empower our future generations with the knowledge and skills needed for fostering a culture of lifelong learning and well being. We believe that focusing on preventive care and education can have a ripple effect that will positively impacts individuals, families and societies at large.

We feel that ensuring holistic development of a nation entails prioritizing both health and education. These pillars are integral to the prosperity and competence of any nation on a global scale. Recognizing that economic growth is only achievable with robust health – both physical and mental – our mission is to provide accessible healthcare and educational resources to communities worldwide. By reducing the burden of diseases we improve the well being of our populace while cultivating a more innovative and productive workforce. Education furthers our motive of promoting social mobility and reducing inequality, paving a way for a prosperous and equitable society where opportunities abound for all, regardless of their background.
